About Me

Hello! I’m Derrick Sarfo Wiredu — a code-loving creative with a knack for turning raw ideas into clean, user-friendly digital products.

My roots? Graphic design. But somewhere between pixels and passion, I discovered my love for frontend development and backend logic. Today, I build full-stack web apps using tools like HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, Flask, and MongoDB.

I’m currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering at UMaT. But trust me — my learning doesn't stop at lectures. I spend late nights hacking on side projects, from smart dashboards to this very portfolio.

I don’t just write code — I solve problems, design smooth UX, and bring bold ideas to life. Whether it’s collaborating on a team or shipping solo, I’m here to build things that matter.
I believe great software is born where creativity meets clean engineering.

Let’s connect, create, and maybe even launch the next big thing together.
